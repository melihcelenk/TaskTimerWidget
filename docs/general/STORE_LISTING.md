## Short Description (English) - Max 200 characters
Track your time efficiently with TaskTimerWidget - a minimalist desktop timer for managing multiple tasks. Simple, clean, and always visible on your desktop.

## Description (English) - Max 10,000 characters (Plain text for Store)

TaskTimerWidget is a lightweight, elegant desktop widget designed to help you track time across multiple tasks effortlessly.

KEY FEATURES

Simple Task Management - Create, rename, and delete tasks with just a few clicks
Real-Time Timer - Track elapsed time for each task with precision
One Active Task - Focus on what matters, only one task runs at a time
Visual Feedback - Active tasks are highlighted in gold for instant recognition
Percentage Display - See how much time each task takes relative to your total time
Drag & Drop Reordering - Organize your tasks by simply dragging them
Right-Click Rename - Quickly rename tasks with a context menu
Always Visible - Compact widget stays on top of your desktop without being intrusive
Data Persistence - Your tasks and times are automatically saved locally
Offline First - Works completely offline, no internet required
Clean UI - Minimalist design with smooth interactions

PERFECT FOR

Developers tracking time on different projects, freelancers managing billable hours, students organizing study sessions, and anyone who wants to understand where their time goes.

PRIVACY

100% Local - All data stays on your device
No Tracking - We don't collect any analytics or telemetry
No Ads - Clean interface with no advertisements
No Account - Start using immediately, no sign-up required

HOW IT WORKS

1. Click the + button to create a new task
2. Click on a task to start/pause the timer
3. Click another task to switch - previous task auto-pauses
4. Right-click to rename tasks
5. Drag tasks to reorder them
6. Your data is saved automatically

See exactly how you spend your time with percentage breakdowns showing each task's share of your total tracked time. TaskTimerWidget features a clean, modern interface with dark task cards on a light background, gold highlighting for active tasks, smooth hover effects, and a compact window that won't clutter your workflow.
